Medical Imaging Research Prerequisites

  1. Medical Imaging Systems : X-ray imaging, CT, MRI, UCA, and basic Ultrasound imaging
  2. Ultrasound Imaging : From 3D wave equations to Hystotrypsy
  3. Biostatistics : Pre-requisite for any empirical research really; t-tests, ANOVA, normality tests, correlation, and more.
  4. Causal Inference : When just correlation isn’t enough and you want to make a statement; Expected Outcomes, DiD, TWFE, DAG analysis, confounding and colliding factors.
  5. Computer Vision : Essential for Image processing; SIFT, Edge and corner detection, convolutional filters, image stitching (Homography), etc.
